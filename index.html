About Me

Hi, my name is Dustyn Doyle. I am currently a Web Developer working at LaunchDM. I have been working professionally as a Web Developer for the past four years, all at LaunchDM. I became interested in Web Development in High School when I took a Web Design program. After High School I continued my education at Harrisburg Area Community College (HACC), in the Web Development and Design program. In that program I focused in Development and furthered my interest to become a Web Developer professionally. Since graduating college I have primarily developed WordPress sites using the Genesis framework. Along with developing with WordPress I have also contributed, published a plugin (Simple Google Analytics Tracking), and attended multiple WordCamps (Lancaster, Philadelphia, and US).
